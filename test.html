1) Topcoder : DiceThrows (Probability , DP)
Let ,
a[i] = Probability of getting sum ‘i’ for player A.
b[i] = Probability of getting sum ‘i’ for player B.
Here , i can be any valid sum according to the constraints. (1 – 20000). To calculate a[i] for all i , we can use DP. DP states should be , *steps (how many times person A need to throw that dice) and *sum (means I). b[i] can be calculated same way. So the the probability of winning A = ∑ 1 20000 ( a [ i ] ∗ ( 1 − ∑ 1 i − 1 b [ i ] ) ) . Here , we can use cumulative sum technique to reduce time complexity.
